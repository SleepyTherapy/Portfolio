About Me

My name is Nevaeh. I am a 16-year-old artist who primarily specializes in traditional art, with growing experience in digital illustration and design. I began drawing at a very young age, learning how to hold crayons and pencils around the age of two, and I have continued to develop my skills consistently ever since.

Art has always been more than a hobby for me—it is a lifelong passion and career goal. My work is driven by a desire to create expressive, meaningful pieces that bring happiness and inspiration to others. I officially began pursuing art professionally in January 2024, focusing on commissions, portfolio development, and sharing my work with a wider audience.

Experience

I have experience working at the intersection of art and business. I have previously owned two small businesses, participated in a large-scale art project, and completed multiple independent art projects.

Several of my artworks have sold within minutes of being released, earning over $300 in total sales. These experiences strengthened my skills in pricing, client communication, time management, and delivering high-quality work under deadlines.
