About Me

My name is Therapy (Nevaeh in real life). I am a 16-year-old artist who primarily specializes in traditional art, with growing experience in digital illustration and design. I began drawing at a very young age—learning how to hold crayons and pencils around the age of two—and have continued developing my skills ever since.

Art has always been more than a hobby for me; it is a lifelong passion and career goal. My work focuses on expressive storytelling, character design, and emotional visuals meant to bring happiness and inspiration to others.

I officially began pursuing art professionally in January 2024, focusing on commissions, portfolio development, and sharing my work with a wider audience.

Experience

I have experience working at the intersection of art and business. I have previously owned two small businesses, participated in a large-scale art project, and completed multiple independent art projects. Several of my artworks have sold within minutes of being released.

Through my creative work, I have earned over $300 in total sales, gaining valuable experience in pricing, client communication, time management, and delivering high-quality work under deadlines.
